## 📖 About TripStar

TripStar (旅途星辰) is an AI-powered travel assistant designed to help you plan trips without stress. It cuts through the overload of travel information and helps you make decisions simply and clearly.

Whether you want to organize your itinerary, find places to visit, or set up your travel plans, TripStar guides you through each step.

---

## 🖥️ System Requirements

To run TripStar on your Windows computer, it should meet these requirements:

- Windows 10 or later (64-bit recommended)  
- 4 GB of RAM or more  
- 2 GHz dual-core processor or better  
- At least 200 MB of free disk space  
- Active internet connection (some features require online access)  

Make sure your system matches these specs for smooth use.

## 🛠️ Installing TripStar

1. After downloading, find the installer file in your Downloads folder. It usually looks like `TripStarSetup.exe`.

2. Double-click the installer file to start the setup.

3. The setup wizard will open. Follow the prompts:

   - Choose the installation folder or keep the default.
   - Agree to the license terms.
   - Click **Install**.

4. Wait while the installer copies the files. This can take a few minutes.

5. When it finishes, click **Finish** to close the wizard.

TripStar is now installed on your computer.

## ▶️ Running TripStar

1. Find the **TripStar** shortcut on your desktop or in the Start menu.

2. Double-click the shortcut to open the program.

3. On first startup, TripStar may ask permission to access the internet. Allow it for full features.

4. The main screen will appear with simple options to start planning your trip.

## 📝 Using TripStar for Trip Planning

TripStar helps by breaking down the process into clear steps:

1. **Enter your destination and dates**  
   Type where you want to go and your travel dates.

2. **Pick interests**  
   Choose what you like: museums, food, nature, history, or entertainment.

3. **Review suggested spots**  
   TripStar will offer places and activities based on your choices.

4. **Build your itinerary**  
   Add places to your daily plan. You can reorder or remove items anytime.

5. **Save and export**  
   Save your itinerary or export it as a PDF for easy access on your trip.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

If you have issues starting or running TripStar, try these steps:

- Restart your computer and try running TripStar again.
- Make sure your Windows is up to date.
- Verify that your antivirus or firewall does not block TripStar.
- Check your internet connection.
- Reinstall TripStar if necessary (download the latest installer again).

For more help, look for the “Help” section inside the app.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Data

TripStar only collects data needed to improve your travel planning experience. Your itinerary and preferences stay on your device unless you choose to share them.

Internet access is used to fetch live travel info, such as weather and attraction details.
